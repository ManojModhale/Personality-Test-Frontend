Openness to Experience

This trait reflects a person's inclination towards creativity, imagination, and intellectual curiosity...

    • Are open-minded and eager to explore new ideas, concepts, and experiences. Enjoy artistic expression, appreciate beauty, and seek novelty.
      Tend to be more flexible in their thinking and adaptable to change. Conversely, individuals low in openness:
      Prefer familiarity and routine over new experiences.
      Might be more conventional, practical, and cautious about change.
      Might display less interest in abstract or unconventional ideas.
